## Final review
### HERO
- The hero headline is now a single compact statement.
- Hero text is short and focused.
- Upload box remains the main focus and is presented prominently.
- Desktop and mobile spacing is balanced for a professional SaaS converter look.

### POPULAR TOOLS
- The section now shows core converter pairs only:
  - JPG → PDF
  - PNG → JPG
  - PDF → Word
  - DOCX → PDF
  - MP4 → MP3
- It appears as a quick-access converter grid, not a category menu.
- Cards are compact, equal-height, and minimal.

### SPACING
- Reduced whitespace between homepage sections.
- Lowered card height and padding across the page.
- Homepage content fits within 1-2 scrolls on a large desktop viewport.

### WHY CHOOSE
- Kept 4 small cards.
- Cards are low height and concise.
- The section is visually compact and not heavy.

### HOW IT WORKS
- Presented as a light, 3-step flow.
- Copy now reads: Upload → Convert → Download.
- Steps are minimal and not overdesigned.

### MOBILE CHECK
- Headline and text are not breaking awkwardly.

- Navbar is compact and clean.
