## Approval checklist
- HEADER: logo is clear, menu is minimal
- HERO: upload box is the center of attention, hero height is compact, text is short
- POPULAR TOOLS: converter cards are visible and not category-style documentation
- SECTION: card count is modest and whitespace is restrained
- MOBILE: no overflow, text is legible, buttons are tappable

## Recommendation
Proceed without further homepage edits for this phase. If a next step is needed, focus on subtle brand refinement only: tighter text hierarchy, button emphasis, and hover states. Avoid adding any new homepage sections or major layout changes.
