## Current Coverage

Current documented converter coverage is centered on:
- Video: MP4 to MP3

- Document: PDF to JPG, JPG to PDF, Word to PDF

Current landing-page patterns already support:
- converter-specific hero sections
- upload-led CTA flows
- benefits and FAQ blocks
- related-tools linking

This creates a strong base for expanding the converter catalog with high-intent, linkable, and monetizable tools.

## Recommendation

The strongest near-term traffic opportunity is to expand around high-intent, high-link-value converter pairs that already match Converigo’s current product DNA:
- audio conversion
- image optimization
- PDF transformation
- document export

The best first-wave rollout is:
- MP4 to WAV
- MP3 to AAC
- WAV to MP3
- JPG to WebP
- PNG to WebP
- WebP to JPG
- PDF to Word
- PDF to PNG
- DOCX to PDF
- PPTX to PDF

These tools combine strong user intent, clear supporting content, strong internal linking possibilities, and a natural fit for landing pages that already exist in the current product structure.
