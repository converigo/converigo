What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, improve functionality, and understand how visitors use the site.

How Converigo uses cookies

Converigo may use cookies to support essential site functionality, remember basic preferences, and collect anonymous usage data that helps us improve the experience. Cookies also help us understand which pages are most useful to visitors.

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to operate correctly. They enable core functions such as page navigation, security, and basic site performance.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website. They may collect information such as page views, referral sources, and general session behavior in an aggregated form.

Advertising cookies

If advertising is enabled on the site, third-party providers such as Google AdSense may use cookies to deliver relevant ads and measure their performance. These cookies may be used across sites in accordance with the provider’s own privacy and advertising policies.

Google AdSense disclosure

Our website may display advertising through Google AdSense or similar services. These services may use cookies and related technologies to personalize ads and analyze ad performance. Please review Google’s privacy and advertising policies for more information about how data may be collected and used.

Managing cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality or limit some features of the service.

Contact information

If you have questions about our cookie practices or this Cookie Policy, please contact us through the contact page or the support channel available on our website.
