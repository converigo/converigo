Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Converigo collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit our website and use our file conversion services. We are committed to handling your data transparently and responsibly.

Information we collect

We may collect information that you provide directly, such as your email address if you contact us, as well as technical information automatically generated by your browser and device, including IP address, browser type, operating system, referral source, and usage data.

Uploaded file processing

When you upload a file for conversion, we process that file solely to provide the requested conversion service. We do not use uploaded files for unrelated purposes, and we do not share them with third parties except as required to operate the service securely and efficiently.

Temporary file storage and deletion

Uploaded files and generated output may be stored temporarily while processing is in progress. We aim to remove temporary files promptly after conversion is completed, failed, or after a reasonable retention period. In some cases, storage duration may vary depending on system performance, error handling, or operational safeguards.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, remember preferences, and support basic site performance. You may be able to adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, although some features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website, which pages are most useful, and how we can improve the experience. Analytics data is generally collected in aggregate form and may include information such as page views, device type, and general traffic patterns.

Advertising and Google AdSense

Our website may display advertisements, including advertising provided by Google AdSense or similar third-party services. These providers may use cookies, web beacons, and related technologies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other websites across the internet. If advertising is enabled, such providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security, and you should take care when sharing sensitive content.

Your rights

You may contact us if you have questions about the information we hold about you or if you would like to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information where applicable. We will respond to legitimate requests in a reasonable timeframe.

Contact information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our contact page or the support channel provided on our website.
